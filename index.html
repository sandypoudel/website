About Me

Hi, I’m Sandesh Poudel. I’m from Bharatpur, Chitwan. I did my high school at Orchid Science College, and I’m currently in the final semesters of my Bachelor’s in Computer Science at Birendra Multiple Campus. I’m passionate about technology, especially web development and programming. I’m currently working on hands-on projects to grow my skills and explore the world of tech one step at a time. This website is my personal space to share what I’ve built, what I’m learning, and what’s next.

My Work

I’m currently the President of the NSU Birendra IT Club, where we organize hackathons, seminars, and practical IT programs. The club helps students gain real-world experience by encouraging hands-on learning, project collaboration, and skill-sharing. Our aim is to support and guide tech students beyond the classroom through events that make learning practical and engaging.

Alongside my academic journey, I’m also active in student politics as a member of the Nepal Student Union. As a student leader, I focus on making college life smoother for others—whether it’s helping with administrative tasks, offering academic guidance, or simply being someone students can reach out to when they need support. I believe in being approachable and doing what I can to improve the student experience from the inside out.
